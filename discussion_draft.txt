## 标题 / Title

【开发方向征集】接下来更新什么，由你来定（What's next? You decide!）

---

## 描述 / Description

### 中文

大家好，感谢大家对这个 Mod 的支持。

目前的版本已经实现了查询配方、查看物品信息、图标悬浮详情等核心功能，整体来说已经比较完整了。

接下来的一段时间，我个人的开发精力会转移到另一个新项目上，但这**不意味着这个 Mod 停更了**。正好相反——我希望接下来的更新方向由你们来定。

如果你有什么想要的功能，或者遇到了什么 Bug，请在这个讨论下面回复。我会把每一条反馈都记录下来，定期整理并安排开发。换句话说：**这个 Mod 的更新速度，取决于你们的反馈有多少。**

一些可以参考的方向：
- 还缺什么功能？（比如全量检索、掉落来源、配方收藏等路线图中的项目）
- 和哪些 Mod 有兼容性问题？
- 现有功能的细节可以怎么优化？

哪怕只是说一句「这个 Mod 很好用」，我也很开心。所以不要犹豫，有什么想说的尽管留言。

感谢每一位订阅和使用的人 🙏

---

### English

Hey everyone, thanks for the support!

The mod is currently in a pretty solid state with core features like recipe lookup, item info, and hover details all working well.

I'll be shifting my focus to a new project for a while, but **that doesn't mean this mod is abandoned**. Quite the opposite — I want YOU to decide what comes next.

If there's a feature you've been hoping for, or a bug that's been bothering you, just reply to this discussion. I'll read every single comment, log them all, and prioritize development based on what the community wants. In other words: **how fast this mod updates is up to you.**

Some ideas to get you thinking:
- Missing features? (Full item browser, drop/source locations, recipe bookmarks, etc.)
- Compatibility issues with other mods?
- Any small tweaks that would make your experience better?

Even a simple "this mod is great" means a lot. So don't be shy — drop a comment!

Thanks to everyone who subscribed and used the mod. You're the reason I made it 🙏
