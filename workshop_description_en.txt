[h1][JEI] What Is This[/h1]

[b]Hover over an item and press R to see how to craft it, or U to see what it can be used for. Check the Info tab for full item stats. A JEI-like recipe lookup tool for Don't Starve Together.[/b]

Inspired by Minecraft's JEI (Just Enough Items), this mod adapts the same concept to DST's unique design patterns.

We've all been there — picking up an unfamiliar item with no idea what it's for, flipping through the crafting menu for ages trying to find where something comes from, or standing at a cook pot with a pile of ingredients wondering what recipes are even possible. This mod solves all of that.

[h1]Features[/h1]

[list]
[*] [b]R Key — Source Lookup[/b] — Hover any item and press R to see all recipes that produce it (crafting + cooking)
[*] [b]U Key — Usage Lookup[/b] — Hover any item and press U to see what recipes it can be used in
[*] [b]Info Tab (New!) [/b] — A brand new Info tab showing comprehensive item stats:
  [list]
  [*] Food: Hunger/Health/Sanity restored, food type, temperature effects
  [*] Combat: Attack damage, range, armor absorption
  [*] Equipment: Equipment slot, speed bonus, sanity per minute
  [*] Tools: Action type, efficiency multiplier
  [*] Misc: Uses, spoilage time, burn/fuel duration, waterproofing, insulation, max stack, trade value, repair materials, sewable status
  [/list]
[*] [b]Cooking Conditions[/b] — See exact tag requirements for every dish (e.g., "Meat > 1", "Monster = 0"), sourced from the official Wiki
[*] [b]Real-time Inventory Match[/b] — Ingredients you have are highlighted, missing ones are marked in red
[*] [b]Smart Substitution[/b] — Running low on an ingredient? The mod automatically finds legal substitutes in your inventory
[*] [b]One-Click Cook Pot Fill[/b] — Open a cook pot and click to auto-fill all ingredients at once
[*] [b]Cross-Navigation[/b] — Click any ingredient icon to look up its own recipes (left-click for source, right-click for usage)
[*] [b]Crafting Panel Jump[/b] — Click the craft button next to a recipe to jump directly to the game's crafting panel
[/list]

[h1]How to Use[/h1]

Hover over any item and press R to see where it comes from, or U to see what it can make. The popup has three tabs: Crafting, Cooking, and Info.

Click any ingredient icon to drill deeper — left-click to see how it's made, right-click to see what it's used for. The craft button on crafting cards jumps to the game's crafting panel. The auto-cook button on cooking cards fills your open cook pot when you have enough ingredients.

Press R or U again (with nothing under the cursor) to close the popup.

All hotkeys can be customized in the Mod Configuration screen.
