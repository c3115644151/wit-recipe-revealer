[h1][JEI] What Is This — DST Recipe Lookup Tool[/h1]

[b]Hover over an item and press R to see how to craft it, or U to see what it can be used for. Check the Info tab for full item stats. A JEI-like recipe browser for Don't Starve Together.[/b]

Inspired by Minecraft's JEI (Just Enough Items), this mod adapts the same concept to DST's unique design patterns.

We've all been there — picking up an unfamiliar item with no idea what it's for, flipping through the crafting menu for ages trying to find where something comes from, or standing at a cook pot with a pile of ingredients wondering what recipes are even possible. This mod solves all of that.

[h1]Features[/h1]

[list]
[*] [b]R Key — Source Lookup[/b] — Hover any item and press R to see all recipes that produce it (crafting + cooking)
[*] [b]U Key — Usage Lookup[/b] — Hover any item and press U to see what recipes it can be used in
[*] [b]Info Tab[/b] — Full stats panel covering food, combat, equipment, tools, and misc
[*] [b]Cooking Conditions[/b] — Exact tag requirements for every dish (e.g., "Meat > 1", "Monster = 0")
[*] [b]Cross-Navigation[/b] — Click any ingredient icon to dig deeper (left-click = source, right-click = usage)
[*] [b]Crafting Panel Jump[/b] — Jump directly to the recipe in the crafting panel
[*] [b]Interactive Key Rebinding[/b] — Click a key button in config, press any key to rebind instantly
[/list]

[h1]How to Use[/h1]

Hover over any item and press R to see where it comes from, or U to see what it can make. The popup has three tabs: Crafting, Cooking, and Info.

Click any ingredient icon to drill deeper — left-click for source, right-click for usage. The craft button on crafting cards jumps to the game's crafting panel. The fill button on cooking cards auto-fills an open cook pot when you have enough ingredients.

Press R or U again (with nothing under the cursor) to close. All hotkeys can be customized in the Mod Configuration screen, accessible via the ≡ button in the top-right of the popup.
